Naval War

Command your warship. Dominate the seas.

Single Player Action

Naval War is a single-player tactical combat game. Command your ship through dangerous waters, destroy enemy fleets, and claim your place on the Local Leaderboard.

Battle against AI-controlled ships, defensive structures, and hostile drones. Your progress (last score and top high scores) is saved locally.

Tips for Survival

  • Physics Matters - Ships and bullets interact with islands. Don't get stuck!
  • Dodge Tanks - Tank shells ignore terrain at first, but you can hide behind other islands.
  • Kill to Heal - Leveling up restores your health.
  • Leaderboard - Your best scores are saved. Can you reach #1?
When you die, your score is recorded to the Hall of Fame. Hit 'Respawn' to try again!
